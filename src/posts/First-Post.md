## Why I Decided to Build My Own Portfolio

Having used various GitHub templates for my portfolio in the past, I realized I wanted more control and a deeper understanding of how everything works under the hood. As someone deeply in love with SvelteKit and Svelte, it was a natural choice for me to use these technologies to build something truly my own.

## The Journey Begins: From Templates to Customization

It all started with a realization—I've been using templates created by others for far too long. While they were great for showcasing my projects, they lacked that personal touch. I wanted my portfolio to reflect not just my skills but also my personality, my passion for learning, and my love for elegant solutions.

## Choosing SvelteKit: A Love Story in Code

When it came to choosing the right technology stack, there was no hesitation. SvelteKit, with its reactive components and seamless transitions, captured my heart. The decision to dive deeper into SvelteKit wasn't just about following a trend; it was about embracing a framework that resonates with my approach to web development—fast, efficient, and delightful to work with.
