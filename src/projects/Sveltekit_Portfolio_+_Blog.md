## From Templates to Customization

Having used various GitHub templates for my portfolio in the past, I realized I wanted more control and a deeper understanding of how everything works under the hood.  While they were great for showcasing my projects, they lacked that personal touch. I wanted my portfolio to reflect not just my skills but also my personality, my passion for learning, and my love for elegant solutions.

## Technologies used

1. **Svelte + SvelteKit**: The backbone of the project, offering powerful features and a smooth development experience. 
2. **mdsvex**: For reading and rendering markdown files.
3. **Shiki**: For syntax highlighting, providing a visually appealing way to present code snippets.
4. **Phosphoricons**: For sleek and consistent icons throughout the site.
5. **Tailwind CSS**: For rapid and efficient design, ensuring the site looks great on all devices.
6. **GSAP**: For animations, adding a dynamic and engaging layer to the user experience.
